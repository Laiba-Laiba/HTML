WEB PROGRAMMING

Web programming is an essential skill in today’s digital world, allowing developers to create interactive and dynamic websites. By learning how to connect HTML pages with folders, you can organize your project files in a structured way, making your code cleaner and easier to manage.

The folder structure in web development usually contains separate directories for HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and media files. Linking an HTML page with these folders ensures that all resources like stylesheets, scripts, and images load properly when the webpage is opened in a browser.

TEA
Tea is a soothing and refreshing beverage enjoyed by people all around the world. Made by steeping dried leaves of the Camellia sinensis plant in hot water, it comes in many varieties such as green, black, oolong, and herbal teas. Beyond its comforting taste, tea is also valued for its health benefits, including antioxidants that support overall wellness. Whether sipped in the morning for energy or in the evening for relaxation, tea holds a special place in daily life and culture.
